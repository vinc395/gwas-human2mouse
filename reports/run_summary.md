Major caveats:
- selected study files are preserved locally under downloads/<study_accession>/ and parsed from those local copies
- all retained human coordinates are standardized internally to GRCh38/hg38 before human BED generation
- GRCh37 selected files without harmonised GRCh38 input are lifted to hg38 feature intervals before downstream BED generation and documented in build_conversion.tsv
- interval/CNV rows are retained as exact intervals rather than collapsing them to 1 bp SNPs
- missing or readme-only studies are logged and skipped rather than crashing the run
- cross-species liftOver maps coordinates, not disease causality
